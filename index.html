Privacy Policy - FemPulse

Last updated: October 7, 2025

Welcome to FemPulse. FemPulse is a menstrual-cycle companion that helps you track periods, phases, symptoms, reminders, and wellness tips. Protecting your privacy and keeping your health data secure are top priorities for us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

This Privacy Policy outlines:

  • What data we collect and why.
  • How we store and protect your data.
  • Your rights and choices.
  • How to contact us.

1. Data We Collect

1.1 Account & Authentication

When you sign up or sign in, FemPulse uses email verification with a 4-digit OTP (one-time passcode). We collect:

  • Email address (used to deliver the 4-digit OTP and account messages)
  • Hashed authentication tokens and metadata used by our authentication provider
  • Password reset flow also uses a 4-digit OTP sent to your verified email

1.2 Profile & Cycle Data

To provide cycle predictions and personalized insights we collect:

  • Full name (optional)
  • Start date of the previous month (used to seed cycle calculations)
  • Cycle duration (period length) and interval_days (cycle interval)

1.3 Logs, Symptoms & Usage

Features that collect health / behavior entries:

  • Calendar entries and phase highlights (dates and phase labels)
  • Logs you add: physical symptoms, behavioral notes, mood entries
  • Health tips requests and the symptoms you logged (used to generate relevant tips)

1.4 Reminders & Notifications

If you enable reminders, we store your preferences for:

  • Period start reminders
  • General reminder preferences (e.g., drink water)
  • Notification history (in-app)

1.5 Location

FemPulse does not require or collect location data for core cycle features.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the information we collect to:

  • Authenticate and secure your account .
  • Calculate cycle phases, next phase, next period and other date-based predictions based on your start_date, duration and interval_days.
  • Show the home page summary: edit period options, current phase, next phase, next period and cycle snapshot.
  • Populate and highlight your calendar by phase.
  • Store logs (symptoms, mood, behavior) and generate tailored health tips and insights.
  • Provide insights (cycle analytics, symptom counts) and reminders.
  • Improve app performance through aggregated analytics and crash reports.

3. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell your personal or health data. We may share data with:

  • Service providers who perform services on our behalf.
  • Analytics providers (in aggregated or pseudonymized form) to help us improve the app.
  • When legally required (e.g., to comply with lawful requests) or to protect rights and safety.

Any third party that receives personal data on our behalf must follow contractual obligations to protect it.

4. How We Store & Secure Data

We implement reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect your data, including:

  • Encrypted communications (HTTPS/TLS).
  • Secure authentication.
  • Access controls and role-based permissions for backend systems.
  • Regular patching and security reviews.

While we strive to protect your data, no system is completely secure — if a breach occurs we will notify affected users as required by law.

5. Sensitive Health Data

Logs of symptoms, moods, menstrual cycle details and similar data are sensitive. By using FemPulse and entering such information, you consent to our collection and processing of that data for the purposes described (personalized tips, calendar features, insights). We treat these entries with additional care and limit access to the minimum necessary personnel and systems.

6. Your Rights & Choices

You can:

  • View and update your profile information from Settings → Edit Profile.
  • Edit period data (duration, interval) from Settings → Edit Period.
  • Export or request deletion of your account and data — contact us using the details below.
  • Opt out of promotional messages and adjust reminder notifications in Settings.

7. Children

FemPulse is not intended for children under 13. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 13 without parental consent, we will delete it promptly.

8. Insights, Aggregation & Research

We may analyze anonymized and aggregated data (for example, symptom frequencies or aggregate cycle statistics) to improve FemPulse and to create non-identifiable insights. Such aggregated data cannot be linked back to an individual.

9. External Links & Resources

FemPulse may provide links in the app to external reputable resources (e.g., PCOS guidelines, menstrual hygiene tips, period myths) hosted by third parties such as government health sites or public medical organizations. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those external sites — review their policies before sharing information.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. When changes are material, we will notify you in-app or by email and update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of FemPulse after changes indicates acceptance of the revised policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions, requests to export or delete your data, or other privacy concerns, contact us:

  • Email: fempulse.health@gmail.com
  • In-app: Use the "Email Us" section in Settings.

Thank you for trusting FemPulse with your health information. We’re committed to protecting your privacy and helping you manage your cycle with confidence.
